Songbird Adventures

“Songbird Adventures” is a literacy through music program designed to support, encourage and enrich the foundations of literacy. Children will love the original books series that walks them through instrument building, language and literacy, rhythmic/ melodic improvisation. The following is a list of links between music and literacy that are included in the program:

  • Rhythm
  • Prosody
  • Phrasing
  • Memory Skills
  • Aural Discrimination
